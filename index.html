            • Validation roadmap — how this becomes a certified product
            1
            Live standards API integration
            Connect Common Core API, state DOE feeds, NAEP framework documents. Replace AI knowledge proxy with authoritative, versioned data. Est: 8 weeks engineering.
            2
            Parallel validation pilot
            One school, synthetic audit vs real student outcomes, 1 semester. Measure correlation. Without this data the product stalls at the curriculum director level.
            3
            Learning scientist advisory
            Hire or partner with a credentialed instructional designer to validate the audit rubric. Required to pass district procurement. Eliminates the internal skeptic blocker.
            4
            FERPA + COPPA compliance
            Legal review before any district contract. Student data laws are non-negotiable. Get DPA templates prepared before first district sales call.
            5
            Calibration flywheel
            Feed real student outcome data back into audit scoring model. Every school that uses it improves accuracy for all. This is the moat a standalone AI query cannot replicate.
